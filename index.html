Hi, I'm Lisa.

As a freelancing Atlanta-based designer/illustrator, I have over ten years’ experience creating visually appealing illustrations, logos, printed ephemera, and websites for various small- and medium-sized businesses and nonprofits. After graduating from Georgia Tech’s UX | UI Bootcamp in June 2022, I hope to join your team as a newly minted User Experience | User Interface Designer and researcher. I want to learn more about the user for whom I am designing, and to use that knowledge to create a visually appealing interface that transcends graphic design.
